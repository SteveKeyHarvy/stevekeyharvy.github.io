About Me

Hi, my name is Stephen Harvey, and I am currently a Sophomore at The University of Dayton. I am studying Business Administration with a Major in Management Information Systems and Finance, as well as a Minor in Computer Science. I like being active on campus and am currently involved in Flyer Consulting, The Hanley Sustainability Fund, Flyer Agriculture, and Club Tennis. Along with these associations I have a high interest in equity portfolio management, and algorithmic trading. Also, I am committed to serving the community, which is the main reason I joined Flyer Consulting, as well as the main reason I co-founded Flyer Agriculture, which I am currently presiding over.

Organizations Flyer Consulting

Flyer Consulting is a University of Dayton student-run organization that aspires to provide complimentary and specialized business development strategies for the non-profit sector in the Dayton community.

Organizations Hanley Sustainability Fund

The Hanley Sustainability Fund is a student-run equity portfolio investing in stocks of companies showing “green” initiatives. HSF employs a non-discretionary algorithmic model which uses different valuation and balance sheet measures, and a discretionary branch which uses traditional fundamental analysis.

Organizations Flyer Agriculture

Flyer Agriculture's mission is to advocate for the growing of sustainable and organic produce; through this, Flyer Agriculture commits itself to leading all agriculture based projects on the campus of The University of Dayton; as well, Flyer Agriculture maintains relationships and acquires produce from local growing partners, with the intent to re-distribute these goods to dining halls and other markets, within The University of Dayton.
